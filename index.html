  • Benford's Analyzer Google Workspace Add-on

    https://workspace.google.com/marketplace/app/benfords_analyzer/434870747252

    • Specifications

    • Achievements

    • Challenges

    • A Google Workspace add-on to enable users to analyze non-zero leading numbers in their Google Sheets documents.

    • Coded in Google App Script, a subset of JavaScript with several unique functions and capabilities.

    • The add-on had to be easy to use and account for various problems that could occur on the user's side.

    • Program had to output useful data and display it unambiguously, as it may be used in fraud detection processes.

    • Managed to create a fully functional add-on that delivers results consistently and quickly.

    • Google App Script was challenging to use at first due to many unique commands not present in vanilla JavaScript.

    • The Google Workspace store's Oauth and other verification processes were difficult to use and were not well documented.

    • The add-on was rejected a few times due to non-conformance with the Google Workspace's policies, which took time to overcome.

    • Use of regex to identify non-zero leading numbers was not very intuitive and human-readable at first.
